Taco Soup!

Description

This Taco Soup recipe is a fall and winter staple! Its an easy, protein packed soup full of zesty, crave-worthy flavor, plus its perfectly hearty and filling. Its like chili's soupier cousin and its loaded with all the best taco style ingredients

ingredients

  • 1 Cup mild salsa
  • 15 ounce can of black beans - rinsed
  • 15 ounce can of pinto beans - rinsed
  • 15 ounce can of corn - drained
  • 14.5 ounce can of petite diced tomatoes
  • 3 table spoons of taco seasoning
  • 2 cups low sodium chicken stock
  • 1 pound of boneless skinless chicken breast
  • Optional toppings: cilantro,plain greek yogurt or sour cream, grated cheese, avocado, green onion, tortilla chips.

Steps

  1. Place chicken breast into the slow cooker and cover entirely with salsa,black beans, pinto beans, corn, diced tomatoes,chicken stock and taco seasoning. Stir to combine
  2. Cover and cook on low for 6 hours.
  3. Remove chicken and shred or cut into bite sized pieces and return to the slow cooker. Stir back into the soup.
  4. Serve with toppings desired.
